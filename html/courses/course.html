About Us

Understand the basic anatomy and physiology of the human body, including the major organ systems and their functions. Explore the principles of medical terminology and develop proficiency in understanding and using medical terms. Gain knowledge of common diseases and disorders, their causes, symptoms, and treatment options. Learn about the importance of preventive medicine and strategies for maintaining good health. This unit will cover topics such as imaging tests (X-rays, MRI, CT scans), blood and urine analysis, genetic testing, and other diagnostic methods. Students will also understand the importance of early diagnosis in medical practice and how these tools play a crucial role in the health and well-being of patients.
